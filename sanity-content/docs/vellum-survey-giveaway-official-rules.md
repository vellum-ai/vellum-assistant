Eligibility:

This giveaway is open to legal residents of the United States who are 18 years or older. Employees of Vellum and their immediate family members are not eligible to participate. This offer is void where prohibited by law. For residents of countries outside the United States a separate prize is detailed out in the “Prize” section.

Sponsor:

The giveaway is sponsored by Vellum dba Vocify Inc., 169 Madison Avenue Unit 2323 New York NY 10016.

How to Enter:

To enter, complete Vellum's 4-minute survey and provide a valid email address in the designated field. Survey completion and a valid email are required to qualify. Limit one entry per person.

No Purchase Necessary:

No purchase or payment is necessary to enter or win.

Entry Period:

The giveaway entry period begins 11/20/2024 and ends 12/31/2024. Entries submitted after the entry period will not be considered.

Winner Selection and Notification:

One (1) Winner will be selected at random from all eligible entries. The random drawing will take place on or around January 6 2025. The winner will be notified via the email provided in their survey submission within seven (7) days of the drawing. If the winner does not respond within five (5) days, an alternate winner may be selected.

Prize:

US residents: If the Winner is a US resident, they will receive a MacBook M4 Pro. Approximate retail value: $2,000.00. The prize is non-transferable and cannot be exchanged for cash or substituted for another item.

Non-US residents: If the Winner is a non-US resident, they will receive $2,000 cash compensation in the form of a virtual Visa/Mastercard gift card.

Taxes:

The winner is responsible for all federal, state, and local taxes associated with the receipt and use of the prize. Vellum may issue a Form 1099 to the winner if required by law.

Privacy:

By entering, participants agree to the collection and use of their information for the purposes of this giveaway, marketing, and survey-related follow-ups, in accordance with Vellum's privacy policy.

General Conditions:

Vellum reserves the right to disqualify any entrant found to be tampering with the entry process or violating these rules. The giveaway is subject to all applicable federal, state, and local laws.

Limitation of Liability:

By participating, entrants agree to release and hold harmless Vellum from any claims or liability arising from their participation in the giveaway or the acceptance and use of the prize.

Void Where Prohibited:

This giveaway is void where prohibited by law.
