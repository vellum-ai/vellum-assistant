Create an agent that checks data processing agreements (DPAs) or privacy policies for compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and other frameworks. • Parse documents and extract references to key obligations (data retention, subprocessor lists, breach notification). • Compare coverage against a compliance checklist. • Output a compliance score, missing elements, and action recommendations.
