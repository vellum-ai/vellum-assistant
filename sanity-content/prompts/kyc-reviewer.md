Create an agent that automates “Know Your Customer” (KYC) checks. • Take customer-uploaded documents as inputs (ID, proof of address, corporate certificates). • Verify document validity, completeness, and expiry. • Flag missing or inconsistent information and recommend follow-up actions. • Output a compliance summary
