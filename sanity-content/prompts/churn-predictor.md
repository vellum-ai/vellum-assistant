Create an agent that analyzes ticket volume and sentiment trends via Slack threads. • Identifies customers at risk of churn based on engagement drop-offs. • Scores each account and adds a “Churn Risk” tag to HubSpot. • Sends a Slack summary to CSMs with recommended re-engagement actions.
