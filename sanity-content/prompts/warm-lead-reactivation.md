Create an agent that scans the CRM for “stale” leads (no activity for &gt;30 days) with positive historical engagement (e.g., opened emails, previous demos). • Generate a short personalized outreach draft referencing the last conversation or interest area. • Suggest next best action (e.g., send new case study, invite to webinar). • Auto-draft message in Gmail for review before sending.
