Create an agent that monitors mentions of our company or key topics across Twitter/X, LinkedIn, and YouTube. • Identify influencer or partner mentions, categorize them (positive, neutral, negative), and record them in a Notion database. • Send a daily digest to Slack summarizing high-impact mentions and recommended engagement actions.
