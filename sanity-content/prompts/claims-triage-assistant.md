Create an agent that triages incoming insurance claims based on completeness and risk. • Parse claim forms, photos, and adjuster notes. • Categorize claims by type (auto, property, life) and priority. • Flag high-risk or complex cases for manual review. • Output structured JSON for claims management system ingestion.
