## Prompt

Every day at 9am, pull a keyword from {{Google Sheets}}, fetch top ranking articles via {{SERP API}}, scrape them with {{Firecrawl}}, analyze themes and structure, add light supporting research, generate a long form SEO optimized article, and save it to a {{Google Doc}} linked back in the original spreadsheet.

## Content

### Why you need it

Writing SEO content that actually ranks takes more than just good writing. You need to understand what already ranks, what topics Google expects to see, and how to structure an article so it covers the full intent behind a query. Doing this manually means opening dozens of tabs, pulling notes, and stitching everything together. This agent automates that work by researching top results, extracting common themes, and generating a clear, well structured article you can edit or publish right away.

## FAQ

### How does the agent research the topic?

It uses web search to review top ranking pages for the keyword or URL, then extracts common themes, headings, and gaps to inform the article.

### What kind of content can it generate?

It can generate full blog posts, SEO articles, outlines, or supporting sections depending on how you configure the output.

### How SEO focused is the output?

The article follows common SEO patterns like clear headings, topic coverage, and search intent alignment, but you can adjust tone and depth.

### Does it replace human editing?

No. It gives you a strong first draft that saves hours of research and writing, but you can easily review and refine it before publishing.

### Where is the final article saved?

The final article is saved as a new page in Notion, formatted in clean Markdown and ready to edit or publish.
