## Prompt

Create an agent that analyzes software earnings call transcripts and turns them into a 5 slide {{Gamma}} presentation I can share with stakeholders.

## Content

### Why you need it ‍

Earnings calls are dense and time consuming to break down manually. This agent reads the full transcript, pulls out the most relevant numbers and narrative insights, and turns them into a clean 4 to 5 slide brief that you can instantly turn into a Gamma deck. It saves hours of review time and helps you share informed insights with your network faster.

### What you need in Vellum

Text input for uploading the earnings call transcript A summarization prompt that extracts highlights Logic to capture both quantitative and qualitative insights Output formatting for a 4 to 5 slide brief Integration or export that hands the brief to Gamma

## FAQ

### How does the agent decide what is important?

It reads the transcript and surfaces themes like financial performance, product updates, guidance changes, competitive positioning, and relevant metrics.

### Does it output a final presentation?

It outputs a concise slide brief that can be passed directly to Gamma, where the presentation is automatically generated.

### Does it capture both numbers and narrative insights?

Yes. It identifies revenue changes, growth rates, margins, and also commentary like strategy shifts, risks, and market signals.

### How long does the output usually take?

Processing time depends on transcript length but is typically only a few seconds once inputs are provided.

### Can I customize the tone or slide structure?

Yes. You can adjust the formatting prompt to change layout, tone, voice, or insight emphasis.
