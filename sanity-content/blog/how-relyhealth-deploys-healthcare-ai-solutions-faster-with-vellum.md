## AI development, at scale

Rely Health’s mission is simple: make sure every patient gets the right care at the right time. But that looks different for every hospital and doctor, which means AI needs to be flexible.

That’s where their Content Engineering team comes in. They work alongside care navigators to understand what parts of their job feel repetitive or manual—and then find ways to automate them.

Today, RelyHealth offers various AI solutions:

Voice agents: Each client gets a list of customized AI-powered phone workflow. These voice agents automate tens of thousands of routine, lower acuity calls for navigation teams (who only get looped in when a patient needs additional help). RelyHealth customizes these calls to the precise needs of the client and navigators workflow. These agents can handle after-hours patient calls every day! Patient check-ins – Automated follow-ups to keep care on track. Smart triaging – Screens patients and seamlessly hands them off to human navigators. Automated charting —Transform clinician-patient interactions into structured EHR data automatically. Additionally, they autonomously identify actionable insights and next steps for their navigators and care teams.

"We create dozens of AI workflows—easily 7-8 per client , covering patient phone calls, provider calls, and more. Vellum has been a huge help because we need to rapidly iterate and deploy. We can take a given execution, drop it into our evaluation table, and keep moving fast." says Prithvi, CTO at Rely Health.

All of these are done with less engineering effort and no delays.

## Debugging In Minutes

Before, if an AI workflow failed, engineers had to manually test each prompt to find the problem. Changing a prompt risked breaking something else. Now, their team uses Vellum’s Observability tools to capture executions in production, replay them and update safely.

“When one of our apps faces an issue in production, a doctor would contact us, and we’d resolve it in under 10 minutes. Vellum’s tracing views made it easy to identify exactly where the problem is," said Natoli.

With Vellum’s observability tools, they can trace errors instantly, fix them, and deploy the update—all in minutes.
