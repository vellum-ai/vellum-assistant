# Who Is This Survey For?

This survey is designed for anyone involved in the AI development lifecycle. Here’s who we’re looking to hear from:

Developers and Engineers: Share your experience building, testing, and deploying AI models. Product Teams: Talk about how you’re prioritizing features, gathering requirements, and delivering AI-driven solutions. Subject Matter Experts (SMEs): Provide insights on aligning AI capabilities with real-world needs. Data Scientists and Machine Learning Practitioners: Discuss how you're selecting and fine-tuning models, and the challenges you're navigating. Leaders and Executives: Share your perspective on strategy, adoption hurdles, and team enablement .

# What’s Inside the Survey?

We’re asking the questions you’ve probably been curious about too:

What’s your go-to tech stack for building AI applications? Are you using fine-tuned models or relying on off-the-shelf solutions? How do you monitor and evaluate your models in production? What challenges have you faced, from data security to managing "hallucinations"? What are you prioritizing for 2025? ‍
